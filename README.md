# Pharma Sales Dashboard

## Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing pharmaceutical sales performance across regions, products, and sales representatives.  
The goal is to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance insights.

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI

---

## Dashboard Visuals

### 1. Total Revenue by Region Over Time
This visual analyzes how revenue evolves across different regions over time.  
It helps identify high-performing regions and seasonal trends and highlights regional sales fluctuations in Q1.

### 2. Total Revenue by Product by Sales Rep
This visual shows revenue distribution across products and sales representatives.  
It identifies top-performing sales reps and best-selling products and evaluates individual performance contribution.

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue performance varies significantly across regions over time.
- Certain regions consistently outperform others in Q1.
- Specific sales representatives contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
- Product performance differs depending on both region and sales representative.

---

## Business Value
This dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by:
- Identifying high-performing regions
- Tracking sales representative performance
- Optimizing product strategy
- Supporting revenue forecasting
